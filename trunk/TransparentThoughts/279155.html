什么是工程？- -

                                      

Ralph Johnson：Art or Science?
http://www.cincomsmalltalk.com/userblogs/ralph/blogView?showComments=true&entry=3265652849
 
“Software development is engineering. Engineering is a creative activity based on artifice and craftsmanship as much as it is on math and science. As the Engineering FAQ puts it, engineering is the bridge between art and science (http://www.tcnj.edu/~rgraham/whatare.html ) Real engineers appreciate beauty and craftsmanship, as well as powerful mathematical models. ”
 
所以我经常说呢，一般鼓吹所谓“科学的软件工程”者，是连工程学都没学及格的。还好我是学工程学的。
