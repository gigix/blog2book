How hot Rails is?- -

                                      

Rails and Zvents on Digital Life TV
 Tyler Kovacs, CTO of Zvents, had a great segment in the DigitalLife TV episode 10 where he got to showcase his Rails application and talk about Ruby on Rails and Ajax in general. Thanks a bunch for the kind words, Tyler. It’s great to see Rails getting some TV coverage.

Ruby on Rails at News.com and ZDNet
 Ruby on Rails chases simplicity in programming is a the title of a very positive Rails article that’s running both on News.com and on ZDNet.

It's so cool then. Have you ever seen a technical framework focused by somehow popular media? So Rails is different.
